# AURA Care - Launch Video Prompt

## Brief Elaborative Prompt

**Create a 60-second cinematic launch video for "AURA Care" - an AI-powered elder care app.**

### Video Flow:
1. **0-6s**: Emotional opening - elderly parent alone at home, warm lighting, dust particles in sunlight, text: "Every 3 seconds, a senior falls at home..."
2. **6-12s**: Phone mockup with medication reminder UI - large buttons, "Time for Medicine" notification, tap to confirm
3. **12-18s**: Split-screen OCR demo - prescription scan with AI extracting medicines, dosages, before/after food instructions
4. **18-23s**: AI voice assistant avatar with animated waves - bilingual Hindi/English support
5. **23-29s**: Caregiver dashboard - stats grid, alerts, senior profile
6. **29-35s**: Video call split-screen - father and daughter connected with heart animation
7. **35-43s**: Feature grid showcase - 6 features with icons
8. **43-50s**: Emotional climax - "Because every moment matters" with trust indicators
9. **50-60s**: Logo reveal - "AURA Care" + "Intelligent Support for Independent Living" + Download CTA

### Style:
- Cinematic, warm lighting, hopeful atmosphere
- Modern minimal UI with large accessible buttons
- Soft background music building to emotional swell
- Sound effects: chimes, scan sounds, connection tones

### Technical:
- 1920x1080, 30fps, 60 seconds
- Brand colors: Indigo (#6366f1), Amber (#f59e0b), Emerald (#10b981)
